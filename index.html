Welcome to Guess the Famous!

A real-time social guessing game, perfect for shelters, waiting rooms, and any group hangout.

How It Works

  1. Join a room with your friends using a shared room code.
  2. Each player secretly picks a famous person.
  3. Try to guess who picked who — out loud!
  4. Capture other players and collect points to win.
